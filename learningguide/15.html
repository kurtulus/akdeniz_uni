CULTURE NOTE

Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills is a famous city in LA, California, which is home to artists, actors, and actresses. It is a place where rich and famous people live and hang out. It is also a tourist attraction.

The Oscar Award

The Academy Awards, commonly known as The Oscars, is an annual American awards ceremony honoring cinematic achievements in the film industry. Winners are awarded the statuette, officially the Academy Award of Merit, which is much better known by its nickname Oscar. The awards, first presented in 1929 at the Hollywood Roosevelt Hotel, are overseen by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences.
