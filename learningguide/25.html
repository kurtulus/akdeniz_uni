Crowdsourcing (all one word) is a relatively new practice of getting a large group of people usually by contacting them on the Internet to do one of several possible things. You could use crowdsourcing to get new ideas or new information from people. You could use crowdsourcing to solve some problem that requires a lot of different people. You could also use crowdsourcing to ask people for money that you need in order to run a certain project or even start a new business that is the use of crowdsourcing we are going to talk about today people who use crowdsourcing to get money for their businesses.
