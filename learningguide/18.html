GLOSSARY

rate (v): officially judging whether a film or TV program suitable or unsuitable for people of a particular age to see.

 

CULTURE NOTE:

As in many other countries, a movie rating system exists in USA. It informs people about the quality of new movies and also informs people if a particular movie is suitable or not for a particular age to see. In Turkey, RTÜK officially judges movies and TV programs.
