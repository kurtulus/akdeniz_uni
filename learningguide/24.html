GLOSSARY

athlete (n):

(a) Someone who competes in sports competitions, especially running, jumping, and throwing

(b) Someone who is good at sports and who often does sports

hurdles (n):

one of a series of small fences that a person or horse has to jump over during a race, such as

the 100 meters/400 meters hurdles

a race in which the runners have to jump over hurdles

code (n) :

a set of rules, laws, or principles that tell people how to behave

Each state in the US has a different criminal and civil code.

The pentathlon is a track event where athletes compete in five different tasks; shooting guns, fancying, fighting using swords, swimming, horse riding and running. The decathlon is a track event where athletes compete in ten different running, jumping and throwing tasks.
