GLOOSSARY

audition (n): a short performance given by an actor, a singer, etc., so that someone can decide whether they are suitable to act in a play, sing in a concert, etc.

She had an audition for drama school.

audition (v): to take part in an audition

audition for

She's auditioning for Ophelia in 'Hamlet'.

 

CULTURE NOTE

American Idol was created based on the British show Pop Idol, which was in turn inspired by Popstars, a show TV producer.  It is like ‘Yetenek Sizsiniz’ in Turkey.
