GLOSSARY

happen to be: to do or be something by chance

This happens to be my favorite restaurant.

survey (n):  set of questions that you ask a large number of people in order to find out about their opinions or behavior

carry out/conduct a survey (=do a survey)

We conducted a survey of parents in the village.

survey (v):  to ask a large number of people questions in order to find out their attitudes or opinions:

Of the 100 companies surveyed, 10 per cent had a turnover of £50m to £99m.
